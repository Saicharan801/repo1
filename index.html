BIO

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, into a family with a strong sporting spirit. His father, Prem Kohli, was a criminal lawyer, and his mother, Saroj Kohli, is a homemaker. Virat grew up alongside his brother and sister, and later married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, with whom he has a daughter named Vamika. Kohli made his international cricket debut in 2008 in an ODI match against Sri Lanka, followed by his Test debut in 2011 against the West Indies. Over the years, he established himself as one of the finest batsmen in the world, known for his aggressive style, consistency, and fitness. As captain of the Indian cricket team, he led India to many memorable victories and set numerous records, becoming one of the most influential cricketers of his generation. He continues to be a key player for India and the Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League.
